Solution Proposal
Tableau-quality analytics with live, in-context collaboration
Business intelligence tools give teams powerful charts — but the insight behind those charts lives in email threads, Slack messages, and meeting notes that no one can find later. Our proposal is a dashboard experience that looks and feels like Tableau, but adds a structured live comment layer directly alongside every chart. Analysts, executives, and team leads annotate what they see in real time — flagging anomalies, recording decisions, and surfacing risks — so the data and the thinking about the data stay in one place, permanently.
Tableau-grade visualisations Live chart annotations Executive summary layer Team-level filtering Audit-ready commentary No context switching
📊
The gap Tableau doesn't fill
Tableau surfaces what the data says. It doesn't capture why a number moved, what decision was made, or who flagged a risk — that context disappears.
💬
Comments live next to the chart
Every annotation is tied to a specific chart, time range, and team view. The right panel becomes a living executive summary — not a separate document.
🔒
Decisions become institutional memory
Tagged as findings, risks, or actions — comments are searchable, shareable, and persist across reporting periods so nothing is lost between reviews.
