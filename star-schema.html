Schema type: Constellation Schema — Two fact tables (FACT_FINANCIALS and FACT_ANNOTATIONS) share common dimension tables (DIM_DATE, DIM_DEPARTMENT), forming a constellation (galaxy) schema — an extension of the classic star schema. FACT_FINANCIALS holds all profitability measures (revenue, COGS, gross profit, EBITDA, net profit). FACT_ANNOTATIONS powers the live chart commentary panel — every comment is keyed to a date and department so it surfaces in the correct team filter view. DIM_SEGMENT carries a self-referential FK to DIM_DEPARTMENT, allowing sub-department drill-down without fan-trap risk on the fact grain.
